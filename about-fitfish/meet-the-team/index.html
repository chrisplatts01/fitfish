Meet the team

Meet the Fitfish team who will be supporting you during
your Fitfish experience

Chris is well renowned both nationally and internationally for her writing, speaking and counselling skills. She is passionate about God and has a massive servant heart.

David brings great enthusiasm and drive to both the fitness and prayer ministry teams from a practical and motivational perspective, proving that no matter your age or current ability you can enjoy getting and keeping fit.

Esther has a breadth of experience coaching in many different environments. She is a great fan of the outdoors, meeting people and experiencing new adventures.

Gaynor is passionate about seeing people reach not only their own goals but fulfilling God’s purpose for their lives. She loves using the great outdoors and exercise as a vehicle for this.

With God's help and guidance Jez has gone from being an overweight, heavy smoker and eater to having a successful inspiring career in the fitness industry. He can't wait to inspire you!

Cath is warm, lively and bubbly and a great encourager. With a fantastic background of PE teaching she really knows how to teach and lead people of all abilities in a fun and encouraging way.

Katy is supportive, warm and insightful bringing 10 years’ experience of coaching to the team in many different environments.

Kat Millar is a highly inspirational exercise professional, offering experience since 2003.

Nick will be offering follow up telephone coaching for Fitfish weekend guests. He is involved in a number of different projects in his role as a coach and facilitator.

Pete will be ministering to us through both his talks in Austria and his massage skills on the weekends. He brings an open, friendly attitude and great spiritual insight.

Tonia has been called to minister to a wide variety of people in different forms. She is a great at making people feel welcome and at home, whatever is going on in their lives.

Tracy is passionate about people believing in and realising God’s promises and walking in the destiny that He has planned for them.

Zoe brings a great amount of experience from the Public Health Sector and is passionate about the link between physical, spiritual and emotional fitness.

Fitfish wouldn’t exist without an amazing team on board who are dedicated to giving you a high quality experience whether you come on the weekends, weeks or seek the expertise of our personal trainers or nutritional advisers

Meet the Fitfish team who will be supporting you during your Fitfish experience

The Fitfish team have exceptional qualifications and experience and will ensure that you are enabled to move forward to serve God more functionally and wholeheartedly.

Caren Benstead

Fitfish Aromatherapist and Holistic Massage Therapist

Caren uses a wide variety of massage techniques in her practise to encourage the body to heal itself and work in a way that it was designed to work by its creator.

As a qualified Clinical Aromatherapist, Carens passion is also fuelled by learning that God has provided means of gentle but effective healing in the plants of the earth. Using organic oils, Caren blends products and oils for clients to support their healing, on a physical, emotional and spiritual level.

Caren became a Christian 4 years ago and is a part of the vibrant Source church in Brackley and loves what being a part of a church family brings to her life and in her role as a wife and mum of three wonderful boys.

In her spare time, Caren loves to be outdoors with her boys, admiring Gods creation, especially when returning home to Wales to enjoy the green hills and sandy beaches.

Chris Ledger

Emotional Fitness: Counsellor and Workshop Leader

Chris is an experienced counsellor and trainer having spent over 20 years working in the NHS. She is a national and international speaker and is involved in Christian training covering emotional issues; has authored 2 books and co-authored 6 books; and is the writer of devotional notes for CWR 'Inspiring Women Every Day.'

She currently has her own counselling business and is able to offer many different tools and deep levels of understanding to help people manage their lives better.

As a Licensed Lay Minister in Greyfriars Church, Reading, Chris leads the Prayer and Healing team and is co-leader of the monthly Prayer Café.

Married to John for over 40 years, she has two married daughters and 5 delightful grandchildren. So there is never a dull moment! As an ex dancer keeping fit is important to her and she loves plain chocolate!

David Elliott

Katy Lyne

Emotional Fitness: Coach
