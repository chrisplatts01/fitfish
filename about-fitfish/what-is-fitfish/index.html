Who we are

So what exactly is Fitfish?

Fitfish is a unique and exciting initiative inspiring, motivating and enabling Christians to get the balance right between physical, spiritual and emotional health. We provide Retreats, Personalised Healthy Eating Programmes (Your Plate of Plenty), Online and Local Support and Resources.

We believe that God has an individual, specially designed, exciting and fulfilling purpose for everyone and that we will be at our most alive and satisfied when we are serving Him in the way that he has intended us to.

We believe that to serve Him to our full ability we need to be balanced and equipped physically, spiritually and emotionally in order to reach our potential in Him and discover MORE of His abundant life, promises, adventure and blessings for us.

We have been led to believe without doubt that God is fully behind and has inspired Fitfish at all stages of its development and that we are reaching a real need in the Christian community in the UK to link physical, spiritual and emotional fitness together and to let God take the lead in this journey.

The team are so excited to be working out some of their God given gifts and potential at Fitfish and we can’t wait to support you in your journey.

So come along on one of our retreats in the countryside, the sun or the snow or make the most of our expertise online.

We look forward to meeting you and supporting you to fulfil your potential and discover MORE of what God has in store for you!
