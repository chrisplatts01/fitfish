Fitfish Days

A great taster of Fitfish in a day, guaranteed to be fun and inspiring!

Sometimes it’s just not possible to commit to a weekend away. So here’s another way to experience the flavour of Fitfish!

Your Fitfish day will look something like this:

09.30 Arrivals and Refreshments
10.00 Introduction to Balanced Living
11.00 Fitfish Cake Break
11.20 Devotion and Worship
12.00 No Sweat Chair and Band Workout
12.45 Lunch
13.45 Workshops with Fitfish GPs, Life Coaches and Counsellors
15.45 Praise Aerobics or Circuits/Running
16.30 Close and Fitfish Snacks
17.00 Finish

If you would like the Fitfish team to come and run a day at your church then please contact us.
