Fitfish days

Bring a taste of Fitfish to your Church or Organisation!

Our residential retreats are always popular with amazing feedback and many repeat guests, but sometimes it’s just not possible to commit to a weekend away. So here’s another way to experience the flavour of Fitfish!

We can work with you to come up with the perfect programme and Fitfish team for your day.

A Fitfish day could look something like this:

09.30 Arrivals and Refreshments
10.00 Introduction to Balanced Living
11.00 Fitfish Cake Break
11.20 Devotion and Worship
12.00 Core and Toning Session
12.45 Lunch
13.45 Workshops with GP or Life Coach
15.15 Praise Aerobics
16.00 Close and Fitfish Snacks

To find out more or to book your Fitfish Day then please contact office@fit-fish.co.uk or speak to Gaynor on 0118 947 0337.
