Massage and Therapy

Light Harmony Holistic Therapy

Restoring harmony and well-being to the body, mind and spirit.

Janine is a Christian, fully qualified Holistic Therapy Practitioner, with comprehensive client and practitioner insurance and accreditation with The Guild of Holistic Therapists. Inspired by her love of essential oils and natural therapies of many years Janine can help you to unwind and feel revitalised after a hard day’s work. Regular massage can also help relieve stress, insomnia, gastrointestinal disorders, depression, muscle tension and those aches and pains often associated with anxiety or muscle strain.

So, if you live in Shropshire we are proud to provide to you a holistic therapy service specialising in Swedish massage, hot stone massage, aromatherapy massage and head massage.

Therapeutic massage therapy is provided for women (men on recommendation) in the dedicated therapy room in Village Drive, Lawley Village (Central Telford) where a tranquil, salon environment has been created to encourage total relaxation.

Do contact Janine to book an appointment, or if you have any questions at all.
