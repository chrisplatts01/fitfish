Life coaching

Esther Ridsdale

Coach (Life/Work/Organisational)

Esther has spent the last 15 years working as a consultant and coach supporting people to clarify what is important to them and make progress towards this. Her passion is in ways of living and working and of organizing that are both effective and reflect universal values. She sees this as an important part of responding to the gospel invitation for ‘life to the full’ for all society.

Esther consults in collaborative strategy planning and improvement, has worked in all sectors and is co-founder of the recently formed Civil Society Forum; a forum to help leaders address the organizational challenges in building civil society.

Esther has completed the International Coaching Federation coach training and has a certificate in counseling. She loves the outdoors and is a qualified ski instructor and mountain leader.

Esther enjoys working with a wide range of people from senior managers to young people supporting the journey as they engage in challenges of life and work. She is based in Southampton and London and works with people both face to face and over the phone.

You can contact her on esther@ridsdales.com or phone 0780 243 0246.
