After Dinner Chocolate Coconut Log

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp coconut oil
  • 4.5 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 100g cranberries
  • 100g goji berries
  • 50g almonds chopped
  • Mint and cranberries/pomegranate seeds to garnish

    • To Mould:

  • 2 plastic throw away cups (the white kind are perfect)

Method

  • Melt the coconut oil for 10 seconds, or until melted, in the microwave
  • Add the cocoa powder, cranberries, goji berries and almonds to the coconut oil and mix
  • Pour the mixture into the two plastic cups
  • Put in the freezer for 20 minutes or until frozen
  • Cut the cups away from the chocolate and arrange on a plate to look like a log
  • Dust with icing sugar/white edible glitter and use the mint and pomegranate/cranberries to garnish
  • Serve with coffee after dinner and use a sharp knife or cheese wire to cut.

Categories

  • Gluten Free
  • Dairy Free
  • Nut Free (replace almonds with extra fruit)
  • Vegetarian
  • Cakes and Desserts

Nutritional info per serving

  • Calories: 154
  • Fat: 9.9g
  • Sat Fat: 6.3g
  • Carbs: 18.8g
  • Sugars: 13.2g
  • Protein: 1.5g
