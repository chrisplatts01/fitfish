Apple and almond breakfast bar

Serves 16

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 100g oats (the plain kind used for porridge)
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 0.5 tsp mixed spice
  • 2 tsps natural Stevia sweetener (such asTruvia)
  • 20g flaked almonds
  • 1 tbsp. flaxseed
  • 2 eggs
  • 6 egg whites
  • 125g cottage cheese
  • 3 eating apples (Pink Lady are ideal)
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 180 deg C/ Gas Mark 4/ 350 deg F
  • Mix the oats, baking powder, cinnamon, spice, stevia, almonds, flaxseed and one apple sliced into small pieces together in a bowl
  • In a jug blend the eggs, egg whites,2 remaining chopped apples, cottage cheese and vanilla essence
  • Mix the blended and dry ingredients together. The mixture should be thick but still runny
  • Pour the mixture into and bake in 2 lined loaf tins in preheated oven for 30mins or until inserted skewer comes out clean
  • Turn onto wire rack to cool, cut into 16 slices, wrap individually in foil and freeze for when needed
