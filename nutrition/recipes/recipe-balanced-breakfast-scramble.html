This is a fast balanced breakfast for the morning. Even quicker if you use a left over sweet potato that has already been cooked, it tastes delicious and feels really substantial. Substitute with whatever leftover veg you have available.

Balanced Breakfast Scramble

Serves 1

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 1 small sweet potato
  • 1 egg
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 leek (or other veg) chopped finely

Method

  • Cook the sweet potato in a microwave
  • Meanwhile chop the leek and fry in a small amount of water in a pan
  • When sweet potato is cooked put it with egg, egg whites (use another egg instead if you would like and adjust calories by approx. + 90) and half the cooked leek into a jug blender. Blend until smooth
  • Pour the blended mixture on top of the remaining leeks in the pan
  • Stir with a wooden spoon or spatula as you would with scrambled egg until cooked through
  • Serve
