A healthy twist on this classic cake which really does pack a nutritious punch

Carrot cake

Serves 12

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 180g oats
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 50g walnut pieces + 25g for top of cake
  • 350g carrots (approximately 2 large or 4 small)
  • 6 large / 180ml egg whites
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 125g low fat vanilla yoghurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 100g finely chopped dates
  • Olive oil spray

    • Method

    • Preheat oven to 180 deg C/Gas Mark 4/350 deg F
    • Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
    • Peel and grate carrots.
    • Blend egg whites, eggs, yoghurt and vanilla essence in a jug blender. Add to dry ingredients along with carrot and dates.
    • Spray olive oil around the edges of a non-stick cake pan â€“ preferably shallow and pour batter in. Top with more walnuts and lightly dusted cinnamon.
    • Bake for approximately 45 minutes, or when an inserted skewer comes out clean.
    • Allow to cool for about 15 minutes before turning onto a wire rack and cutting into slices.
