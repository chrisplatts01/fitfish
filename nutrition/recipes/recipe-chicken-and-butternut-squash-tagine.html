This dish is perfect for no stress entertaining as it can be left in the oven right until it is ready to serve.

One Pot Chicken and Butternut Squash Tagine

Serves 4

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 small butternut squash peeled and chopped into bite sized chunks
  • 2 onions chopped into bite size chunks
  • 20 pitted black olives
  • 4 tsp runny honey
  • 100g no soak prunes
  • 2 cinnamon sticks broken in half
  • 2 tsp dried coriander
  • 1200ml chicken or vegetable stock

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 160 deg
  • Put all ingredients into a casserole dish and put into the oven for 2 hours
  • Serve with green salad or quinoa with raisins stirred through

Categories

  • Gluten Free (with gf stock)
  • Dairy Free
  • Nut Free
  • High Protein
  • Low GI
  • Main Meals

  • Carbs: 32.8g
