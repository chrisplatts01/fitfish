A perfect simple dinner that can be prepared quickly and popped in the oven whilst you get on with other jobs

Grilled chicken breast and roasted vegetables

Serves 1

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow pepper, deseeded and cut into chunks
  • 1 courgette sliced
  • 1 beetroot cut into chunks
  • 1 carrot peeled and cut into chunks
  • 100g sweet potato cut into chunks
  • 2 tsp olive oil infused with garlic (or finely crushed garlic mixed with your olive oil)
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 1 chicken breast

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 200 deg C/ Gas Mark 6 / 400 deg F. Mix the olive oil, balsamic vinegar and thyme together in a small bowl
  • Place all of the vegetables in a large bowl and pour Â¾ of the oil and vinegar mixture over them
  • Shake the vegetable thoroughly in the bowl ensuring that they are all coated with the mixture
  • Place the vegetables on a baking tray and put into the oven for 30- 40 minutes
  • 10 minutes before the vegetables are ready grill the chicken breast (if time is short then bake it alongside the vegetables)
  • Place the chicken and vegetable on a plate and drizzle with the remaining mixture

  • Wheat Free
  • Nut Free

Nutritional info per serving

  • Calories: 343
  • Fat: 11.2g
  • Sat Fat: 1.6g
  • Carbs: 25g
  • Sugars: 5.1g (natural)
  • Protein: 31.9g
