A new invention of the Christmas pudding, made in the traditional way and tasting as traditional as all of the others without the excess!

Can’t Taste the Difference Christmas Pud

Makes 12 slices

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 300g raisins
  • 250g dates, roughly chopped
  • 150g prunes, roughly chopped
  • 200ml boiled water
  • 100g mixed peel
  • 175g oats
  • 125g wholemeal self-raising flour, sieved
  • 50g whole almonds
  • 50g shelled pistachios
  • 1 tsp mixed spice
  • ½ tsp ginger
  • Zest of 2 oranges
  • 175g tinned apples blended to make puree or apple puree
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp agave syrup
  • 2 tbsps. orange blossom water

For the topping:

  • 15g apple puree or tinned apple pureed (remains of tin)
  • 2 oranges
  • 1/3 tsp almond essence
  • 1 tsp agave syrup
  • Mint leaves and cranberries or pomegranate seeds to garnish

Method

  • Put the raisins, dates and prunes into a large bowl and cover with 200ml boiling water
  • Meanwhile lightly grease a 1.5 litre pudding basin and line the base with a disk of parchment paper.
  • Put all of the remaining dry ingredients (peel, oats, flour, nuts, spices) and the orange zest into another bowl and mix together.
  • Add the fruit mixture, apple puree, eggs agave syrup and orange blossom water
  • Mix until everything is combined
  • Spoon the mixture into the pudding basin
  • Cover with a circle of parchment paper and then foil and secure with string
  • Place the pudding on an upturned jar lid in a large pan and pour in hot water until it comes half way up the bowl.
  • Cover the pan and gently steam for 4 hours, topping up the water as needed
  • In the meantime blend the remaining apple puree with the oranges, almond essence and agave syrup and set aside in fridge for topping
  • After 4 hours remove the pudding from the heat and tap out the pudding into a serving dish, top with the sauce and garnish with mint leaves and cranberries/pomegranates
  • OR cool the pudding on a cooling rack and keep in the fridge and reheat as needed by steaming or in the microwave or freeze for up to one month and steam for 30 mins to reheat

Categories

  • Dairy Free (contains eggs)
  • Vegetarian
  • Portable
  • Cakes and Desserts

  • Fat: 5.4g
  • Sat Fat: 0.8g
  • Carbs: 69g
  • Sugars: 34.8g
  • Protein: 7.7g
