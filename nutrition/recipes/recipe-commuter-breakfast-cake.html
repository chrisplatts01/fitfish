One of the Fitfish guests wanted an equivalent to porridge to eat on his commute into London. I created this and he loves it and wanted it shared!

Commuter breakfast cake

Makes 10 slices.
If Your Plate allows you can have more than one to give you a good start to the day!

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 200g porridge oats (the bigger the better)
  • 200g a mix of chopped dates, figs, nuts, seeds and goji berries according to your taste
  • 25g desiccated coconut
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • Â½ tbsp agave syrup
  • Skimmed milk to cover
  • A cup of strong black tea (decaf if desired)

Method

  • Pre heat oven to 180 deg C/gas mark 4/ 350 deg F
  • Mix the oats and nut, seed and fruit mix in a mixing bowl
  • Add the desiccated coconut and baking powder and mix
  • Pour skimmed milk on top of the mix until just covered (like you would cover cereal)
  • Add the tea
  • Soak for up to 20 mins
  • Add the agave syrup and egg and stir all of the ingredients together
  • Add a dash more oats if the mix is too runny
  • Line a rectangular loaf tin and spoon the mixture in
  • Bake for approx. 40-45 mins or until a skewer comes out clean

If you want to pack a real balanced breakfast punch then you could add some protein powder to the mix or eat with some plain natural yogurt to up your protein and to make you feel full until lunch.

Categories

  • Gluten Free (if use gf oats)
  • Dairy Free (contains eggs)
  • Nut Free (if replace nuts with seeds)
  • Vegetarian
  • Breakfasts
  • Snacks
  • Portable
  • Cakes and Desserts

Nutritional info per serving

  • Sat Fat: 0.9g
  • Carbs: 25.2g

  • Protein: 7.8g
