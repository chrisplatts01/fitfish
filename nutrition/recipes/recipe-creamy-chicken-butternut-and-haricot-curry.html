Creamy Chicken, Butternut and Haricot Curry

Serves 4

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp. ground cumin
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 pint boiling water
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 cm finely grated ginger
  • Medium butternut squash, grated
  • 1 tsp. chilli powder (or to taste)
  • 3 chicken breasts
  • 1 400g can haricot beans
  • 1 tsp. garam masala
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Handful chopped fresh coriander

Method

  • Dry fry the ground coriander and ground cumin until it smells good (approx. one minute)
  • Add the, water, onion, garlic and ginger and cook for 2 minutes
  • Add the butternut squash and chilli
  • Bring to the boil and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally and adding more water if necessary
  • Reduce the heat and add the chicken breasts, simmering for 10 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through
  • Add the haricot beans and cook for a further 5 minutes reducing the liquid to a thick sauce consistency.
  • Turn the heat off and add the garam masala, lemon and a handful of chopped fresh coriander
  • Stir and serve with additional veg or wholegrain/brown rice
