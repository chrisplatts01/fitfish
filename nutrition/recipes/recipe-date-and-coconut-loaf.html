A delicious sweet cake with some healthy fats and carbs but no added sugar or unhealthy fats.

Date and Coconut Loaf

Makes 12 slices

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 300g chopped dates
  • 25g desiccated coconut
  • 3 decaf tea bags
  • 225g wholemeal self-raising flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp mixed spice

Method

  • Preheat oven to 180 degrees / gas mark 4.
  • Put the dates, coconut and tea bags in a mixing bowl and cover with boiling water to 1cm above the fruit. Leave for 20mins â€“ overnight
  • Remove tea bags
  • Add flour, eggs, baking powder and mixed spice to the fruit and mix together with spoon
  • Grease a cake tin with spray oil or use greaseproof paper or a silicone tin
  • Pour mixture into tin
  • Bake for about 45 minutes, or until an inserted skewer comes out clean.
  • Allow to cool for about 15 minutes before turning onto a wire rack and cutting into slices.

Categories

  • Dairy Free (contains eggs)
  • Vegetarian
  • Snacks
  • Portable
  • Cakes and Desserts

Nutritional info per serving

  • Calories: 145
  • Fat: 2.5g
  • Sat Fat: 0.3g
  • Carbs: 32.6g
  • Protein 4.2g
