A really simple dessert. The strong ginger masks the fromage frais taste. Add dark chocolate or pistachios for a great dessert for special occasions

Easy as Ginger Ice Cream

Serves 4

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 500g tub low fat fromage frais
  • 8 pieces stem ginger (the type that you buy in balls in a jar with syrup)
  • 1 tbsp syrup

Method

  • Chop the ginger pieces into small slivers in a blender or chopping device
  • Mix the ginger and fromage frais in a bowl
  • Add a tbsp of syrup from the jar
  • Mix well and return to fromage frais tub
  • Place in freezer
  • Every 30 minutes for 4 hours stir through the icecream to stop crystals forming.
  • After 4 hours it will be ready to serve. Or keep in freezer and defrost for an hour before use.
