Fat Free Fruit Loaf

Serves 12

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 350g mixed dried fruit of your choosing
  • 200g wholemeal self-raising flour
  • Strong decaf black tea (about 4 tea bags)
  • 1/2 Tbsp honey (to taste, try to leave out)
  • 1 medium egg

Method

  • Put all of the fruit in a mixing bowl and cover with the tea to 1cm above the fruit.
  • Leave to soak covered for a few hours or overnight if you can.
  • Mix the flour, egg and honey (to taste, try it with none) into the fruit mixture
  • Place mixture in a greaseproof paper lined loaf tin
  • Bake on Gas Mark 3/160 deg for 45-50 mins or until firm and skewer comes out clean
  • Leave to cool on a rack before cutting into 12 slices

Categories

  • Dairy Free (contains eggs)
  • Nut Free
  • Vegetarian
  • Snacks
  • Portable
  • Cakes and Desserts

Nutritional info per serving

  • Calories: 134
  • Fat: 1.7g
  • Sat Fat: 0.5g
  • Carbs: 25.6g
  • Protein: 5.9g
