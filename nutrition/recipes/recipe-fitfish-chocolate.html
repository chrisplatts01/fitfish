Chocolate can be good for you! This healthy take on chocolate is based around coconut oil, which is an essential fat and has loads of health benefits!

Fitfish Chocolate

This amount serves one - duplicate as necessary!

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp coconut oil
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder (no sugar added)
  • 1.5 tsp dried chopped dates

Method

  • Heat the oil in a small container in a microwave for 15 seconds or until it melts and is clear
  • Mix in the cocoa powder and stir until dissolved in the oil
  • Place cling film over a small Tupperware container or ramekin size bowl
  • Spread the dried fruit on the bottom of the container
  • Pour over the oil and cocoa mixture
  • Place in the freezer for approx. 10 minutes or until solid and ready to serve
  • Enjoy!

    • You can experiment with different ingredients such as nuts, seeds, desiccated coconut etc.

  • Sugars: 5.7g (natural)
  • Protein: 0.9g
