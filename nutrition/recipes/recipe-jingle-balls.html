A quick and easy colourful treat that tastes like Christmas!

Jingle Balls

Makes 10 balls

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

(For two balls)

Ingredients

  • 25g pistachios
  • 15g blanched almonds
  • 25g goji berries
  • 25g cranberries
  • 50g figs
  • 1/2 Satsuma juice
  • 1/4 tbsp agave syrup
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Method

  • Place all ingredients in a blender
  • Pulse until all blended and a mouldable texture, adding a drop of water if necessary
  • In the palm of your hand make 10 ball shapes
  • Place on a plate in the fridge to cool for at least 15 minutes
  • Serve (The balls can be kept for up to a week in an airtight container)
