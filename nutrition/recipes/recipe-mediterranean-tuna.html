This is really quick and easy and can be made entirely from store cupboard and freezer ingredients adding the tomatoes if you have them

Mediterranean Tuna

Serves 4

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 4 frozen tuna steaks
  • 2 limes, quartered (or lime juice from store cupboard)
  • 200g (approx.) frozen onions
  • 200g (approx.) frozen sliced peppers
  • 2 drained tins or one jar pitted black olives in brine (approx. 700g before draining)
  • One tin 410g chopped tomatoes
  • 4 stems of tomatoes on the vine

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 180 degrees/Gas mark 4
  • Place the tuna steaks in a casserole dish
  • Squeeze the limes over the tuna and leave the quarters in the dish
  • Sprinkle the onions and peppers over the tuna
  • Cover with the chopped tomatoes and olives
  • Top with the tomatoes on the vine
  • Cover with tin foil and bake for 30-40 minutes depending on how well done you like your tuna.
  • Serve with vegetables and your choice of grain
