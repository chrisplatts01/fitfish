Kat and her friend invented these cookies. They're full of all those lovely warming autumn flavours, but are great at any time of year!

Nic and Kat's autumn cookies

Makes 15 cookies

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potato (approx. 225g)
  • 1 apple, grated (approx 100g)—experiment with Bramley/cooking apple or any eating apple
  • 2 Tbsp/20g date syrup or dates (mashed with a splash of hot water)
  • 1 cup/100g fine oats or oat flour (you can blend oats in processor to make them fine like flour)
  • 1/4 cup/30g vanilla whey protein powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Spray oil

Method

  • Preheat oven to 180 degrees/gas 4.
  • Peel sweet potato, cut into cubes and microwave for 8 minutes.
  • Mash until smooth.
  • Microwave the grated apple for about 3 minutes or until soft.
  • Combine sweet potato, apple and date syrup and stir thoroughly.
  • In a separate bowl, combine oats, protein powder and cinnamon. Add to the wet mixture.
  • Roll into small balls and place on a tray lined with greaseproof paper sprayed with oil. Wet a fork and use it to flatten each ball.
  • Bake for about 20-25 minutes, turning the cookies over halfway through.

Categories

  • Gluten Free (if use gf oats)
  • Nut Free
  • Vegetarian
  • Snacks
  • Portable
  • Cakes and Desserts

Nutritional info per serving

  • Calories: 81
  • Fat: 1.2
  • Carbs: 12.8
  • Protein: 3.9
