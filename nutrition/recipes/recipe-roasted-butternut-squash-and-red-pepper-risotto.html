Roasted Butternut Squash and Red Pepper Risotto

Serves 4

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 175g dried risotto rice
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 6 spring onions sliced
  • 1 clove garlic crushed
  • Â¼ teaspoon cumin seeds or ground cumin
  • 1/2 inch fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 red peppers chopped
  • 850ml vegetable stock
  • 2 heaped tbsp frozen peas

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 200 deg Celsius/400 Fahrenheit or Gas Mark 6
  • Place the butternut squash whole in the centre of the oven and roast for 30 mins whilst you prep the other ingredients
  • In a large pan or saucepan, heat the coconut oil and add the rice. Cook over a low heat for 2-3 minutes stirring all of the time until the rice looks translucent but not brown
  • Add the onion, spring onions, garlic, pepper, ginger and cumin. Cook gently for 4-5 minutes stirring frequently.
  • Meanwhile remove the butternut squash from the oven and chop into bite size chunks (with or without the skin depending on your preference). Add to the risotto.
  • Add a couple of ladles of hot stock. Stir well and then cook on a medium heat for 20-25 minutes, gradually adding the stock a ladle at a time, until the rice is tender.
  • Add the peas, stirring through gently.
  • Season if required and serve.
