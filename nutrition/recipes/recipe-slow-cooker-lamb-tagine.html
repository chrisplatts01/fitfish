Slow cooker lamb tagine

Makes 6 servings

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 320g diced lamb
  • ½ can kidney beans
  • ½ can borlotti beans (or chick peas)
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 red onion sliced
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tsps ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 400g can diced tomatoes
  • 250ml vegetable stock
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 5 dried prunes
  • 5 dried apricots
  • Zest of one lemon
  • 1 tbsp ground oats
  • 1 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • 1 tbsp coriander, chopped
  • Handful spinach
  • Coriander to garnish

Method

  • Into the slow cooker add all but the last 7 ingredients, mix well.
  • Set slow cooker and leave to cook for 8-10 hours
  • Half an hour before serving stir in the remaining ingredients
  • Serve with a mix of bulgar wheat and quinoa with raisins and coriander stirred through or with a large salad.
  • Garnish with coriander.

Categories

  • Gluten Free (if use gf oats or leave out)
  • Dairy Free
  • Nut Free
  • High Protein
  • Low GI
  • Main Meals

Nutritional info per serving

  • Calories: 186
  • Fat: 3.6g
  • Sat Fat: 1.1g
  • Carbs: 24.7g
  • Sugars: 8.1g (natural)
  • Protein: 14.9g
