Sweet potato and protein frittata

Serves 1 for a meal or 2 for a snack

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium- large sweet potato peeled and cut into bite size chunks
  • 1 egg
  • 2 egg whites
  • 2 scoops pea protein powder (20g)
  • 1/2 onion chopped finely
  • 4 heads broccoli chopped finely
  • 4 mushrooms chopped finely
  • Tsp Your favourite spice or herbs
  • Olive oil spray

Method

  • Stir fry the onion, broccoli and mushroom in water until cooked through and softened, add the herbs
  • Meanwhile heat the sweet potato in the microwave for 3-4 mins with some water until soft
  • Put the vegetables, sweet potato, egg, egg whites and 2 scoops pea protein powder in a bowl and mix together with a fork
  • Spray olive oil into the frying pan and heat
  • Making patty shapes out of the mixture place them into the pan
  • Fry for 1-2 mins on each side
  • Serve plain for breakfast and with a salad or vegetables for lunch or dinner
