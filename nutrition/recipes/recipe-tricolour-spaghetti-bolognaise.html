Tricolour Spaghetti Bolognaise

Serves 6

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 3 red onions, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tsp. basil
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • 1 tsp. marjoram
  • 500g lean mince beef
  • 120g dried red split lentils
  • 1 x410g can aduki beans, drained
  • 2 x 410g tins plum tomato
  • 2 tbsp double concentrate tomato puree
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp. reduced salt vegetable stock granules
  • 1 tsp. cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp. vanilla essence

For the spaghetti:

  • 300g dried wholemeal spaghetti
  • 8 carrots thinly sliced into long strands with a peeler
  • 8 leeks cut finely into long strands
  • 4 courgettes thinly sliced into long strands with a peeler

Method

  • In a large pan fry the onions and garlic over a medium heat in a little water until soft (approx. 3-5 mins)
  • Add the basil, oregano and marjoram and stir
  • Add the lean beef mince and fry until browned
  • Add the lentils, aduki beans and plum tomatoes
  • Break the plum tomatoes with the back of a spoon and stir
  • Add the tomato puree, water and stock granules and stir
  • Add the cocoa powder and vanilla essence and stir again
  • Bring to the boil and then turn down the heat and simmer whilst you prepare the spaghetti
  • Cook the spaghetti according to the packet instructions
  • Add the vegetables for the last 3 minutes of cooking
  • Drain the spaghetti and serve with the bolognaise sauce on top

Categories

  • Gluten Free (if use gf spaghetti)
  • Dairy Free
  • Nut Free
  • High Protein
  • Low GI
  • Main Meals
