A delicious lunch or snack, hot or cold. Cut into small squares for a great addition to a picnic lunch. Freezes and reheats and well.

Tuna and Veggie Slice

Serves 12

Your Plate of Plenty Serves

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 250g tub low fat onion and chive cottage cheese or cream cheese
  • 2 large cans flavoured, low fat tuna
  • 1 medium courgette, grated
  • 1 red or orange pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, grated
  • 100g / 8 button mushrooms, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 packet pre-cooked brown rice
  • 1/2 can sweet corn kernels—drained (optional)
  • Juice of 1 small lemon
  • 1 tsp dried dill or mixed herbs
  • 3 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp chicken stock powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spray oil

Method

  • Preheat oven to 180 degrees/gas 4.
  • Whisk eggs and cottage cheese together.
  • Add remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
  • Pour mixture into an oven-proof silicon pan or baking dish sprayed with
  • oil and bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown. Test with a knife
  • Cut into wedges or squares and serve with a fresh salad.

Categories

  • Gluten Free (if using gf oats)
  • Nut Free
  • High Protein
  • Low GI
  • Main Meals
  • Snacks
  • Portable

Nutritional info per serving

  • Calories: 229
  • Fat: 4.1
  • Carbs: 18.5
  • Protein 27.2
