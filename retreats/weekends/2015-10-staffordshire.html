Weekend locations

Come and escape to our beautiful venues in inspiring rural surroundings

Really handy from Birmingham and Manchester, yet tucked away in the countryside, we always have a great time at Shallowford House!

Staffordshire October 2015

Friday 30th October–Sunday 1st November

Shallowford House
Shallowford
Stone
Staffs
ST15 0NZ

We are excited to be going back to warm and welcoming Shallowford House for the fourth time! Join us this autumn and see why this weekend will be so special.

This house is a stunning 19th century building in the rolling Staffordshire countryside. and was originally the private home of the Webster family. The house is situated in five acres of beautifully maintained gardens, woodland and meadow which whilst ideal for outdoor exercise are also perfect places to relax and unwind.

Shallowford House has beautiful single and twin en suite rooms and is located just 3 miles from the M6 (Junction 14) and therefore easily accessible from all parts of the country.

As well as the main accommodation there are lounges, libraries, a chapel and a characterful basement bar!

A weekend packed full of fun and new experiences you will get:

  • 2 nights’ accommodation in a single or twin ensuite room
  • All meals, snacks, and refreshments from Friday evening meal until Sunday lunch. We work with the venue to produce a Fitfish menu.
  • Three sessions from one of our Fitfish Ministry Leaders, worship and ministry time
  • An ‘Emotional Health Option’ to chose from: Life Coaching, Listening Prayer (where you sit in comfort whilst 3 team members listen to God about who He designed you to be) or a Workshop
  • 6 exercise sessions all optional and for all levels, ages and abilities. Come and try something new! There is plenty of space outdoors and indoors for our sessions and a lovely running and walking route too.
  • Workshops and talks on Nutrition and The Benefits of a Healthy Lifestyle giving you handy hints and ideas to take away with you
  • The Fitfish Story – come and hear firsthand how Fitfish started and how God has used it.
  • A repeat guest stream – if you’ve been before we have three new sessions for you!
  • Healthy Extras including sports massage, head massage, foot massage, health assessments and nutrition advice
  • A Fitfish resource pack to explore at your leisure during the weekend or afterwards including goal setting and motivational advice
  • A ‘soaking session’, relaxing in God’s presence and the chance to hear from Him
  • Fitfish team of Personal Trainers, Life Coaches, Nutritional Advisers, Massage Therapists, GPs and Ministry Leaders to chat and pray with.
  • Beautiful rural surroundings and plenty of space indoors and outdoors
  • Quiet times
  • Social times
