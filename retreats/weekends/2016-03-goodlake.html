Weekend locations

Come and escape to our beautiful venues in inspiring rural surroundings

As just 10 rooms are available we expect this boutique barn retreat to sell out quickly, so don’t miss out secure your place today!

Goodlake March 2016

Friday 18th–Sunday 20th March 2016

Goodlake Barns
Church Farm
Shellingford
Faringdon
Oxfordshire
SN7 7QA

Special Offer – stay the Sunday Night too for no extra cost! Return home truly refreshed after a quiet relaxing evening and sleep following the retreat.

This fast selling popular ‘boutique’ spring weekend retreat is jammed packed with new experiences, expertise and resources. Suitable for all ages, levels and abilities. Do as much or as little as you like.

  • 2 nights’ accommodation in a twin or double ensuite or non ensuite room (you can have a room to yourself) Special Offer – stay the Sunday Night too for no extra cost!
  • All Fitfish meals, snacks, and refreshments from Friday evening meal until Sunday lunch. These will be cooked by Sarah and Maggie our excellent Fitfish Cooks who are passionate about Fitfish and good tasting healthy food!
  • Hot tub!
  • Three sessions from one of our Fitfish Ministry Leaders, worship and ministry time
  • An ‘Emotional Health Option’ to chose from: Life Coaching, ‘A Listening Ear’ (A chance to talk something through with a Fitfish Counsellor), Listening Prayer (where you sit in comfort whilst 3 team members listen to God about who He designed you to be) or a Workshop
  • 6 exercise sessions all optional and for all levels, ages and abilities. Come and try something new!
  • Workshops and talks on Nutrition and The Benefits of a Healthy Lifestyle giving you handy hints and ideas to take away with you
  • The Fitfish Story – come and hear firsthand how Fitfish started and how God has used it.
  • A repeat guest stream – if you’ve been before we have three new sessions for you!
  • Healthy Extras including sports massage, head massage, foot massage, health assessments and nutrition advice
  • A Fitfish resource pack to explore at your leisure during the weekend or afterwards including goal setting and motivational advice
  • Fitfish team of Personal Trainers, Life Coaches, Counsellors, Nutritional Advisers, Massage Therapists, GPs and Ministry Leaders to chat and pray with.
  • Beautiful rural surroundings and plenty of space indoors and outdoors
  • Quiet times
  • Social times

Accommodation

These converted barns are very special and we love using this magnificent venue for our ‘boutique barn’ retreats, always receiving excellent feedback.

Only 10 rooms are available and so the whole weekend will be very personal and tailored to your needs. You will feel truly pampered by the 4 and 5 star surroundings in this wonderful rural location.

The barns are a series of light and spacious cottages, sensitively converted and retaining much of the character and charm of the original stone barns, stables and outbuildings. Although we all eat and meet together you will also be able to enjoy the spacious lounges and dining areas in your own cottage.

With under floor heating, exposed beams and many mod cons these barns are a great place to retreat from the busyness of life.

En-suite and non en-suite (with bathroom next door) rooms are available.
