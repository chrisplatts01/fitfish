Come and escape to our beautiful venues in inspiring rural surroundings

We’re delighted to be using the Windmill Farm Conference Centre and we think that we’ve found a real gem in beautiful surroundings. Do come and join us in the Cotswolds!

Cotswolds

Windmill Farm Conference Centre
Main Street
Clanfield
Bampton
Oxon
OX18 2SN

Directions to The Windmill Centre

The Windmill Farm Conference Centre is located less than a mile from the River Thames in the pretty village of Clanfield. Located only 40 minutes’ drive from the major motorways; The Windmill is easily accessible from London, Bristol, Birmingham, and Reading.

The accommodation comprises of spacious, bright twin en-suite rooms which can also be used as singles. The rooms are modern, clean and comfortable and the whole of the centre has recently been refurbished and has a wonderful homely feel There is a spacious lounge in the accommodation area with an open fire and kitchen for limitless drinks, a large spacious dining hall, a conference room and a sports hall with a climbing wall.

Outside there is a mature Cotswold garden and lower farm which may be used as an overflow is a large traditional beautifully kept building located in a mature garden. There is also an orchard, a peaceful space, full of mature fruit trees and a large field.

We’re so pleased to be going to the Windmill in 2012 and we look forward to welcoming you there.
