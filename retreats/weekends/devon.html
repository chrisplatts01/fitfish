A week on the coast in Devon

Come and join us for this amazingly relaxing and inspiring retreat! And what better place to be, right by the sea!

We’re so pleased to be going back to the much sought after Beacon Centre at Lee Abbey. In 2012 we had a fabulous time with 100% of guests saying that they were inspired, that it was value for money and that they will return again! And so do get in quick before it fills as we will keep this one small with just 10 rooms available.

Monday 28th October to Sunday 3rd November 2013

The Beacon Centre
Lee Abbey
Lynton

Directions to Lee Abbey

Take some time out of your busy life to relax and be motivated to make that kick start or boost to your spiritual, emotional and physical fitness whilst being inspired by the amazing surroundings, talks, worship, workshops, quiet times and social times. Do as much or as little as you like and experience a retreat with long lasting benefits.

The week includes:

  • 6 nights’ accommodation in a single, twin, triple or quad room
  • All meals, homemade snacks, fruit and refreshments from Monday lunch until Sunday lunch.
  • Evening worship and ministry time
  • Morning devotions
  • Workshops on Stress, Self-Image, Nutrition, The Benefits of a Healthy Lifestyle, The Components of Fitness, Moving Forward
  • An Emotional Health Option
  • Exercise sessions each day, all optional and for all levels, ages and abilities. Come and try something new or stick to classes that you know that you enjoy
  • Activity sessions including zip wire, mountain boarding, low ropes, archery, climbing, longer walks and more
  • Access to heavily discounted ‘Healthy Extras’
  • Fitfish resource pack to explore at your leisure during the week or afterwards including goal setting and motivational advice
  • A ‘soaking session’, relaxing in God’s presence and the chance to hear from him
  • Evening reflections
  • Testimonies
  • Fitfish team of Personal Trainers, GPs, Life Coaches, Counsellors, Nutritional Advisers and Ministry Leaders to chat and pray with.
  • Campfires
  • Quiet times
  • Social times
  • Follow up support as required

Accommodation

Set amongst 280 acres of stunning coastal estate with its own private beach and a vista of deep blue sea, rugged beaches and lush green valleys the Beacon Centre at Lee Abbey offers the perfect backdrop for our 6 day Fitfish retreat.

The Beacon is finished to a high standard and has been built on the site of a former dairy barn complex with the original beams forming an integral part of the new construction. The bright bunk bedrooms sleep either 1, 2, 3 or 4 guests and have their own toilet and shower right next door in a landing area shared with just one other room. Under floor heating and cosy sofas make this a great place to relax

The grounds are outstanding and provide a fabulous backdrop for our activities, however if the Great British weather gets the better of us we can use the excellent all weather sports hall.
