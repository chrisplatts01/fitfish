Come and escape to our beautiful venues in inspiring rural surroundings

The estate sits in the Chiltern hills above the lovely valley of Hambleden and offers many great walks. The well-known regatta town of Henley-on-Thames is close by with its lovely riverside walks and bespoke shops and restaurants.

Accommodation

As a grade 2 listed building St Katharine’s has kept many features from the Victorian and Edwardian eras and offers a warm welcoming hallway with open fires and cosy lounges. Guests are also greeted by a grand piano and will instantly feel spoilt in this venue.

The grounds provide plenty of variety and include large lawns and a walled garden that is still used today to grow vegetables and fruit for the kitchen.

We are pleased to offer some very special accommodation in St Katharine’s, the manor house. Double and twin rooms with traditional features, furniture and ensuite bathrooms. We also offer single rooms with a toilet and hand basin and a shower room located nearby (1 per person).

The main manor house also provides some comfortable lounges with elegant bay windows looking out onto the historic cedar tree in the grounds and also some panelled libraries.
