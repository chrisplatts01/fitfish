Weekend Retreats

Each Fitfish weekend retreat is jammed packed with new experiences, expertise and resources. Come away and be motivated to make that kick start or boost to your fitness whilst being inspired by amazing views, talks, workshops, quiet times and social times. Suitable for all ages, levels and abilities. Do as much or as little as you like.

All Fitfish weekends include:

  • 2 nights’ accommodation in a twin, double or single room
  • All meals, homemade snacks, fruit and refreshments from Friday evening meal until Sunday lunch.
  • Three talks on being spiritually fit for God, worship and ministry time
  • An Emotional Health Option including Life Coaching, Telephone Coaching, ‘Talking to a Counsellor’, Listening Prayer, Prayer Ministry or an Emotional Health Workshop
  • 5 or 6 exercise sessions all optional and for all levels, ages and abilities. Come and try something new or stick to classes that you know that you enjoy
  • A Nutrition Workshop giving you handy hints and ideas to take away with you
  • Healthy Extras including sports massage, head massage, foot massage, fitness assessments and nutrition advice
  • Extra workshops including Quiz the Trainer, GP Q&A, Goal setting, Music and the Mind, Music to De-stress
  • A Fitfish resource pack to explore at your leisure during the weekend or afterwards including goal setting and motivational advice
  • A ‘soaking session’, relaxing in God’s presence and the chance to hear from him
  • Fitfish team of Personal Trainers, Life Coaches, Counsellors, Nutritional Advisers, GPs and Ministry Leaders to chat and pray with.
  • Inspiring rural surroundings and plenty of space indoors and outdoors
  • Quiet times
  • Social times
  • Follow up support as required
