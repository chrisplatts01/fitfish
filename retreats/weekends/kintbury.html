Weekend locations

Come and escape to our beautiful venues in inspiring rural surroundings

We don’t expect that you’ll need to leave this venue over the weekend but if you do you’ll find the historic traditional market town of Hungerford nearby with its many antique shops, boutique shops and cafes. The Kennet and Avon canal passes through the town and there are walking and cycling paths running alongside.

Kintbury

St. Cassian's Centre
Wallingtons Road
Kintbury
Hungerford
RG17 9SR

Directions to St Cassian's Centre

We’re delighted to be using Kintbury as a Fitfish venue and hope that you’ll enjoy the location as much as we do. St Cassian’s (or Kintbury as it is known) is located in many acres of beautiful Berkshire countryside and there’s a mix of conservation areas, more formal gardens and a small lake to enjoy.

We use some of these for our workouts and at other times you will no doubt find them inspiring and relaxing.

We are pleased to provide twin rooms at Kintbury which can also be solely occupied. The rooms are simple but comfortable and offer great value we believe. Each room has a washbasin as well as storage space and a desk and chair.

Showers, toilets and washbasins are located near to the bedrooms.

Kintbury offers many varied areas for relaxing, catching up over a cuppa or having a post work out snack, including a comfortable lounge and relaxed homely kitchen area

There’s also a magical quiet room in the loft with bean bags and candles and a chapel on the ground floor which you may want to use.

Other nooks and crannies can be discovered all over the house which you can escape to in addition to the lovely grounds of course.
