Weekend locations

Come and escape to our beautiful venues in inspiring rural surroundings

We’re delighted to be spending a week in this stunning location and we do hope that you will come and join us in one of the most beautiful places in Britain!

Rydal Hall

Rydal Hall
Rydal
Ambleside
Cumbria
LA22 9LX

Rydal Hall offers a warm and friendly home from home in the heart of the English Lake District. A stunning location situated in 34 acres of woodland, Rydal Hall was once the home of the Le Fleming family before being sold to the Diocese of Carlisle in the 1960s. Beautifully restored over many years, the Hall and the surrounding estate and buildings are a luxurious place to stay for a week in The Lakes.

The Estate includes the restored Thomas Mawson garden, over 34 acres of woodland including a free flowing beck which produces the majestic Rydal waterfalls (made famous by William Wordsworth). An early 19th century woodland path leads to the Community Vegetable Garden set in the Old Walled Kitchen Garden. It includes an orchard planted with 30 northern varieties of apple dating back to the 1850s and a plethora of flowers and vegetables growing alongside each other.

With wonderful views over the Rothay Valley, with the Fairfield Horseshoe and Nab Scar as a backdrop, Rydal Hall is the perfect place for a Fitfish week long retreat!

As well as the normal Fitfish activities we will be making the most of the location and include walking and pottering around the beautiful Lake towns in our optional programme.

Walking could include gentle strolls around the garden, to more adventurous walks on public footpaths or out on the fells. The venue makes an ideal starting point for a section of the well-known Coffin Trail – the picturesque pathway from Grasmere to Ambleside.

The single, twin or double bedrooms are all en-suite and beautiful and homely.

After busy days we can relax in the traditional bar inside the venue with stone floors and an open fire

Come and join us for this really special week in a special place!
