Directions to Shallowford House

Shallowford House is a stunning 19th century building in the rolling Staffordshire countryside. and was originally the private home of the Webster family. The house is situated in five acres of beautifully maintained gardens, woodland and meadow which whilst ideal for outdoor exercise are also perfect places to relax and unwind.

Shallowford House has beautiful single and twin en suite rooms and is located just 3 miles from the M6 (Junction 14) and therefore easily accessible from all parts of the country.

As well as the main accommodation there are lounges, libraries, a chapel and a prayer room.
