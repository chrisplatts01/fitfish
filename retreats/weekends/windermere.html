The Windermere Centre

The Windermere Centre
Lake Road
Windermere
LA23 2BY

Come and join us in this great town based location in the Lake District. The centre is just a 10 minute walk from the idyllic towns of Windermere and Bowness and from Lake Windermere itself.

A great place to spend a bank holiday weekend with cosy space for relaxation inside, a beautiful garden, indoor exercise space should the weather get the better of us and access to some of the greatest scenery in Britain!

All rooms are en-suite and you can share a twin or double (just one of those so get in quick!) or have a room to yourself.

Why not extend your weekend before or after your Fitfish experience and stay at the centre itself or in one of the local B&Bs?

A great choice for your first or one thousandth experience of The Lakes!
