Fitfish France

An inspiring, motivating and relaxing week in the beautiful Vendee region

We are delighted to be taking Fitfish to France for the second time and have found a perfect spot at the villas at La Pas Option, the Spring Harvest Holidays site.

France June 2016

13th–20th June 2016

Le Pas Opton
1335 Route du Pas Opton
85800 Le Fenouiller
France

Come and join us for a fantastic week in this beautiful area of France!

We will be occupying the 3 villas on the Spring Harvest Holidays site of Le Pas Opton.

We will be running our own Fitfish programme with 3 meals a day, exercise sessions, talks, worship, life coaching, listening prayer, visits to local towns, the beach, cycle rides, kayaking and more. There will also be plenty of opportunity to sample some of the Spring Harvest Holidays programme and all of their facilities including the swimming pool, bar, café, ministry times, worship and special events at this wonderful resort.

The villas are spacious and welcoming with plenty of room for socialising and quiet too. We will all eat together in the main villa and we will make the most of the warmer weather (we hope!) with plenty of salads, fruits and barbecues.

Exercise will be varied and take into account the climate with sessions in the shade and the pool and also featuring local bike rides and kayaking. As ever all activities are optional and you will be able to make the most of the Fitfish team and expertise during your holiday.

The area

The nearest town is St Gilles-Croix de Vie, a beautiful coastal town straddling the River Vie (river of life). Here you’ll find supermarkets, shops, restaurants, cafes, bars and opportunities to experience French culture. The town also features the spectacular Grande Plage, 3.5km of unspoilt coastline.

From the harbour in St Gilles, you can take a short boat trip to the idyllic Ile d’Yeu, an offshore island where time stands still. Superb beaches, ideal for exploring on foot, by bike, bus or by rented motor bike.

A classy retreat with traditional huts and miles of pine forest backing on to the beach – it’s a special get-away and somewhere delightful to spend a day.

We will visit St Gilles a couple of times during the week and there is also a shuttle bus to and from the resort every day.

Travel (At your own expense)

See the Spring Harvest Holidays site for travel options.

Last year most people flew to Nantes and couple got the train. A shuttle is arranging from Nantes costing approx £45 return.

Once booked we will add you onto the Fitfish France Facebook page where you can discuss travel with other guests.
