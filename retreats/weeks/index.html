Samos with Richmond

Come and be inspired by guaranteed sunshine, spectacular surroundings and fantastic activities

Do something different for your summer holiday this year and refresh yourself physically, spiritually and emotionally at this highly rated hotel!

Samos

28th August–4th September 2014

Why come back from your summer holiday feeling bloated and unfit? Instead come back fitter and more refreshed!

Fitfish are delighted to be joining forces with Richmond Holidays the provider of quality Christian holidays this year in Samos, Greece!

In addition to the normal Richmond programme of events Gaynor Burton, our founder and director, will be leading two exercise sessions a day (according to people’s interests and fitness levels and including circuits, bootcamp, stretching, flexibility, core stability, aerobics, water based workouts and resistance band workouts)

Gaynor will also run a nutrition workshop during the week and will be available for one to one PT sessions, Fitness Assessments, Nutrition Advice and Motivational Coaching (all payable in resort).

Time for recovery, rest and relaxation is an important part of physical, spiritual and emotional fitness and these resorts allows for plenty!

To book and for more information about the resort contact Richmond Holidays.

We look forward to seeing you in the sun!
