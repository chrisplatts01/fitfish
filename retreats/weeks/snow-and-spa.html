Snow and Spa in Austria

For those who ski and those who don’t…

Snow and Spa January 2016

16th–23rd January 2016

PRICES HELD FROM 2015

Come and join us at the wonderful luxurious Hotel Gürtl, in Haus im Ennstal just an hour from Salzburg and right next to the slopes.

For those who don't ski

  • Leisurely breakfast
  • Swimming pool and whirlpool
  • Sauna
  • Steam room
  • Reading and lounger area with panoramic views
  • Sleigh rides*
  • Cross Country skiing*
  • Winter walks: along the river to and around local villages and towns
  • A train trip to Salzburg*
  • A trip up the mountain to meet the skiers for lunch*
  • Plenty of time to potter, chat, watch, pray, read

For those who ski & board

  • Gondola 1 min walk
  • Ski back to the hotel
  • 6 day lift pass to enjoy Styria’s four biggest skiing mountains
  • 45 gondolas and lifts and 125 kilometres of piste
  • Piste Map here
  • Variety of off piste skiing going up to 2015m
  • A variety of pistes for all level of skier
  • Lessons for all levels can be arranged*
  • 3 afternoons ski guiding*
  • Drying room
  • Lunch* at a different mountain hut each day or ski down to meet non-skiers for lunch

For all

  • 7 nights in spacious en-suite accommodation
  • Buffet breakfast with eggs, bacon, cereals, hams, cheeses, fruit, yogurt, pastries, juices..
  • 3 course dinner + unlimited salad buffet bar
  • Champagne Breakfast
  • Gala Dinner
  • Swimming pool and whirlpool
  • Swimming ‘treadmill’
  • Saunas
  • Steam rooms
  • Reading and lounger area with panoramic views
  • Spa Treatments and Massage*
  • Bar and Lounge
  • Fridge in room
  • Hairdryer
  • Pamper pack with bathrobe, slippers and bag
  • Wi-Fi
  • Boot and Clothes Drying Room
  • A quiet village setting, right next to the slopes, with tourist information, ATMs, supermarket and cafes and bars
